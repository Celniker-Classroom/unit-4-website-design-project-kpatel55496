Fantasy Book Reccommendations

Welcome to Romance! Below you will find some of my reccommendations below.

The Summer I Turned Pretty


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

The Summer I Turned Pretty by Jenny Han is a nostalgic coming-of-age story following sixteen-year-old Belly, who finds herself caught in a love triangle with the two Fisher brothers, Conrad and Jeremiah, during a pivotal summer at Cousins Beach. The novel beautifully captures the emotional rollercoaster of first love and the bittersweet transition from childhood to adulthood, all set against a sun-soaked, dreamy beach backdrop. If you love heartfelt, fast-paced romances that make you feel nostalgic for your own teenage years, this captivating series start is an absolute must-read.

Better Than The Movies


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

Better Than the Movies by Lynn Painter is a charming YA rom-com about Liz Buxbaum, a hopeless romantic who enlists her annoying next-door neighbor, Wes Bennett, to help her win over her childhood crush. As the pair schemes and fake-dates, Liz is forced to reexamine her scripted view of love and realize that the true romance might be right in front of her. Filled with witty banter, nostalgic 90s movie references, and a slow-burn romance, this book is an unmissable, heartwarming read for anyone craving a perfect feel-good story.

If He Had Been With Me


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

If He Had Been With Me by Laura Nowlin is a heartbreaking YA romance that explores the what-ifs of a childhood friendship between Autumn and Finny that fell apart in high school. Despite taking different paths, they feel a magnetic pull toward each other until a tragic accident leads to a devastating conclusion that you know is coming from the very first page. You absolutely must read this emotional, "unputdownable" story to experience the powerful journey of love, loss, and the devastating "what-ifs" that will stay with you long after the final page

A Thousand Boy Kisses


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

A Thousand Boy Kisses by Tillie Cole is a heart-wrenching young adult romance that follows Poppy and Rune, childhood soulmates who make a pact to fill a jar with 1,000 "heart-bursting" kisses. When a terminal illness threatens to cut their time together short, the couple embarks on an emotional journey to live, love, and cherish every single moment before it's too late. If you want a deeply moving, unforgettable tearjerker that will make you appreciate the beauty of life and love, this book is an absolute must-read.

My Life With The Walter Boys


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

My Life with the Walter Boys by Ali Novak is a heartwarming YA romance that follows Jackie Howard, a driven New York City teenager whose life is turned upside down when she moves to a chaotic Colorado ranch with her guardian's twelve rambunctious sons. As she adjusts to her new life, she finds herself trapped in a passionate love triangle between two very different Walter brothers, forcing her to choose between the safe, intellectual choice and the risky, magnetic bad boy. It's an unputdownable, bittersweet story filled with humor, pranks, and raw emotional growth that will make you laugh, cry, and cheer for Jackie as she learns that perfection isn't the only way to find love.

You've Reached Sam


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

You've Reached Sam by Dustin Thao is a heart-wrenching YA contemporary novel about Julie, a seventeen-year-old girl grappling with intense grief after her boyfriend, Sam, dies in a tragic accident. Desperate to hear his voice one last time, she calls his cellphone, but is shocked when Sam answers, giving them a magical, temporary second chance to connect and say goodbye. If you are looking for a deeply emotional, cathartic read that explores the complexities of love, loss, and learning to grow around your grief, this tear-jerker is an absolute must-read.

Anna and the French Kiss


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

Anna and the French Kiss by Stephanie Perkins follows Anna Oliphant, a high school senior unexpectedly shipped off to a boarding school in Paris, where she navigates a new culture, new friendships, and a complicated crush on the charming, but taken, Étienne St. Clair. As they explore the City of Light together, the novel perfectly captures the butterflies, jealousy, and romantic tension of falling in love when you least expect it. It is an absolutely addictive and witty read that will make you feel like you are strolling through Paris, making it a perfect, heartwarming escape.
