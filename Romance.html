Welcome to Romance! Below you will find some of my recommendations.

The Summer I Turned Pretty

Belly navigates a love triangle with two brothers during a pivotal summer at Cousins Beach.

Better Than The Movies

Liz Buxbaum enlists her neighbor to help win her childhood crush, navigating romantic mishaps and witty banter.

If He Had Been With Me

Autumn and Finny's childhood friendship resurfaces with tragic consequences in this emotional story.

A Thousand Boy Kisses

Poppy and Rune attempt to fill a jar with 1,000 kisses while facing terminal illness and emotional challenges.

My Life With The Walter Boys

Jackie navigates life on a chaotic ranch with 12 rambunctious boys while caught in a love triangle.

You've Reached Sam

Julie connects with her deceased boyfriend Sam through a magical, temporary second chance to say goodbye.

Anna and the French Kiss

Anna navigates love, friendships, and a complicated crush at a Parisian boarding school.
