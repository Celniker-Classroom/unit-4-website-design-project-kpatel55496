Welcome to Mystery and Thriller! Below you will find some of my recommendations.

A Good Girl's Guide to Murder


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

Pippa reinvestigates a closed murder-suicide case for her school project, convinced the alleged killer was innocent. Digging into her small town's dark secrets puts her own life at risk.

The Inheritance Games


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

Avery unexpectedly inherits billions from a stranger and must move into a secret-filled mansion to find out why. A high-stakes puzzle game with suspicious heirs and hidden family secrets awaits.

One of Us is Lying


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

Five students enter detention and one doesn't make it out alive — now the other four are suspects. A gripping whodunit blending high school drama with clever twists.

We Were Liars


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

Cadence tries to piece together a forgotten traumatic event from a summer on her family's private island. A beautifully written psychological thriller with a shocking twist ending.

Truly Devious


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

True-crime fan Stevie attends an elite Vermont academy to solve a notorious 1930s cold case. When a present-day murder surfaces, the past and present collide in a twisty mystery.

The Naturals


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

Cassie has a natural talent for profiling and gets recruited into a secret FBI program with other gifted teens. Together they solve cold cases while a new serial killer raises the stakes.

Five Survive


(Click on the image to find the amazon link to the book!)

Six friends' road trip turns deadly when their RV is trapped by a sniper demanding a hidden secret. One terrifying night forces them to turn on each other as buried truths surface.
