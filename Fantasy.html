Powerless

In a kingdom where those without magical abilities are hunted, a powerless girl must survive among elites.

Caraval

A magical game where nothing is what it seems and danger hides behind every illusion.

Once Upon a Broken Heart

A girl strikes a deal with a dangerous immortal to stop a wedding—and enters a world of magic.

Shatter Me

Juliette Ferrars, a teenager locked away because her touch is fatal, must decide whether to be a weapon for a corrupt government or fight back.

Cruel Prince

Jude, a mortal girl in the treacherous High Court of Faerie, navigates political games and vicious fae to find her place.

Dance Of Thieves

Kazi, a former street thief turned elite guard, navigates dangerous games with Jase, the leader of a powerful outlaw family.

Six of Crows

Six teenage outcasts attempt an impossible heist in the city of Ketterdam, filled with action, emotion, and unforgettable character dynamics.

Divine Rivals

Two rival journalists, Iris and Roman, fall in love through magical letters while covering a war between gods.
